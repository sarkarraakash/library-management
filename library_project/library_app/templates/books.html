Book Details
To Kill a Mockingbird

Author: Harper Lee

Description: "To Kill a Mockingbird" is a novel by Harper Lee set in 1930s Alabama, narrated by Scout Finch. It follows her and her brother Jem as they witness their father, Atticus Finch, defend a black man accused of raping a white woman. The story explores themes of racial injustice, moral growth, and empathy.

Book Details
Pride and Prejudice

Author: Jane Austen

Description: "Pride and Prejudice" is a novel by Jane Austen, first published in 1813. The story follows the main character, Elizabeth Bennet, as she deals with issues of manners, upbringing, morality, education, and marriage in the society of the landed gentry of early 19th-century England.

Book Details
1984

Author: George Orwell

Description: "1984" is a dystopian novel by George Orwell, published in 1949. It is set in a totalitarian regime where every aspect of life is controlled by the government. The story follows Winston Smith, who works for the government and begins to rebel against the oppressive regime.

Book Details
The Great Gatsby

Author: F. Scott Fitzgerald

Description: "The Great Gatsby" is a novel by F. Scott Fitzgerald, first published in 1925. It is set in the Jazz Age on Long Island, New York, and follows the story of Jay Gatsby and his obsession with the wealthy Daisy Buchanan. The novel explores themes of wealth, love, and the American Dream.

Book Details
The Shining

Author: Stephen King

Description: "The Shining" is a horror novel by Stephen King, first published in 1977. It tells the story of Jack Torrance, an aspiring writer and recovering alcoholic, who becomes the winter caretaker of the isolated Overlook Hotel in Colorado with his family. As the winter snow traps them in the hotel, supernatural forces begin to influence Jack, driving him to madness and violence.

Book Details
The Hobbit

Author: J.R.R. Tolkien

Description: "The Hobbit" is a fantasy novel by J.R.R. Tolkien, published in 1937. It follows the journey of Bilbo Baggins, a hobbit who embarks on an adventure to reclaim a treasure guarded by the dragon Smaug. Along the way, he encounters various creatures and faces challenges that test his courage.
